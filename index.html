Welcome!

Raised to Life is a North American Based Starcraft II clan open to players of all skill levels. The main purpose of this clan is to provide its members with a friendly community focused on improving at the game. Whether you want to get promoted on the 1v1 ladder, need to practice a new build, or you just need someone to play arcade games with, this is clan aims to help you.
